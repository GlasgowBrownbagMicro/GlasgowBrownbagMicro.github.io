The Club's Convenors

Moritz Mosenhauer, President

Biography

I am a student of the Ph.D. in Economics program at the University of Glasgow under the supervision of Prof. Sayantan Ghosal and Prof. Yiannis Vailakis. My research focus lies in Behavioural Economics. Specifically, I investigate how people allocate their attention using theoretical and empirical methods.

In September 2015, I graduated from the Master in Economics program at the Ludwig-Maximilians-University in Munich. My master thesis "(Non-) Salience under cognitive constraints: a theoretical investigation of the boiling frog" was supervised by Professor Florian Englmaier. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree in East-Asian Economics and Politics from the Ruhr-University in Bochum.
